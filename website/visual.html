Take Notes Visually

Your mind isn't full of sentences and paragraphs, so why should your notebook be? Notewise stays true to the way you think by capturing what's actually in your mind: ideas, and the ways they relate to each other. It lets you express all your thoughts in a flexible, multidimensional way that does justice to your mind's amazing capacity to process information.

The Notewise paradigm is an extension of certain theories of modern psychology (known as "semantic networks" or "concept maps") that describe human knowledge as a vast web of interconnecting ideas. By recording knowledge in this form, you stay truer to your own thought process, and can better reflect the numerous associations between ideas that your mind perceives.

So tap into the visual thinking patterns your brain natually uses, and let Notewise archive what's really on your mind.
