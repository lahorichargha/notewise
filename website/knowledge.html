General Knowledge

Notewise is a tool for storing and organizing knowledge. Common uses like to-do lists and driving directions are just examples of the more general task of expressing and recording whatever you're thinking about. Notewise is most powerful when you use it to represent the complex ideas you come to know: either non-trivial groups of facts that you learn, or your own perceptions and designs. Using Notewise can be a life-changing experience as you make tangible and breathe life into the very thoughts that make you who you are. You'll simply discover more about what you know and what you want to know. Try it!
