Technical Procedures

Whether a programmer, manager, teacher, or salesperson, you inevitably have to familiarize yourself with the mundane details of your organization. Use Notewise to capture these pesky details so you can return to them at will without having to "relearn."

Policies and procedures...the list of managers who have to sign off on a particular form...logon IDs and passwords...computer commands and menu traversals...instructions for submitting vacation requests... It can be overwhelming to keep up with it all. Store it in Notewise and return to any item with just a stroke of the keys.
