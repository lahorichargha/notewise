Project Planning

Use Notewise to help you grasp the ever-changing relationships between people, projects, and tasks. Keep an up-to-date snapshot of what everyone's working on, drawing attention to problem areas, identifying underallocation and overallocation of resources, and brainstorming what will come next. Face it, your mind doesn't conceive of your project as a line-by-line list of bullet items, but as a multi-dimensional endeavor with possibilities extending ever outward. Capture this image and refine it with Notewise's visio-spatial paradigm.
