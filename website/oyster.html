The World is Your Oyster

Information is flooding towards you from all directions, vying for your attention. But your mind is full of so much more than just this cacophony. It also contains your unique perspectives on it all: the conclusions you've drawn, the patterns you've perceived, the opinions you'll defend. Notewise preserves these personal viewpoints, and encourages you to develop them. It helps you construct a web of your own personal knowledge, not simply a grab bag of other people's thoughts. With Notewise, you will better formulate your own views and understandings, and bring clarity to your emerging ideas.
